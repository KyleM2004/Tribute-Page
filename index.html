Sir Tim Berners-Lee

Sir Tim Berners-Lee is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web, web browser, HTML and hypertext transfer protocol. He is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium which oversees continued development of the web. He is also a senior researcher and holder of the 3Com founder's chair at the MIT Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Labratory and was knighted ion 2004 for his pioneering work.
